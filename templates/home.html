Weather Data API

For a station on a specific date: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/{station}/{date}

Example: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/10/1988-10-25

For a station on all available dates: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/{station}

Example: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/10

For a station throughout a specific year: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/yearly/{station}/{year}

Example: http://127.0.0.1:5001/api/v1/yearly/10/1999
